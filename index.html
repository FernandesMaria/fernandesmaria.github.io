About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the CritiX group at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security and Trust (SnT), in Luxembourg. I defended my PhD in May 2020 from university of Luxembourg, under the supervision of Prof. Paulo Esteves-Veríssimo.
