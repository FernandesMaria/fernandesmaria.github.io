About Me

I am a Postdoc at NNF Centre for Basic Metabolic Research, University of Copenhagen. Currently, my work focus investigating the shared genetics between type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease. Before I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Computational Rare Diseases Genomics (CRDG) group based at the Big Data Institute - University of Oxford, in United Kingdom, studying small open reading frames (smORFs), and links with human diseases. My interests lie in human genetics, human diseases, non-coding regions, high-throughput technologies and data privacy.

Research Interests

Human genome-health relationships

Privacy-preserving genomic data analysis

Biomedical data (omics and health records) processing
